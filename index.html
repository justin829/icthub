About ICT Hub Nyamira

ICT Hub Nyamira is a community-driven training center offering short, practical courses to youth across Nyamira County. We focus on hands-on learning in web development, digital marketing, mobile app development, and basic IT skills to help learners get jobs or start businesses.

Our short courses

  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Basic Computer Skills & Office
  • Graphic Design (Canva & Photoshop basics)
  • Mobile App Development (Flutter / React Native)
  • Digital Marketing & Social Media
  • Intro to Data Analytics
